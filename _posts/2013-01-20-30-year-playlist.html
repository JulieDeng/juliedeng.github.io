20 Year Music Journey

I wanted to understand who I was, where I came from, and why I became who I am now. To aid the meditation I compiled a playlist of the songs that made the biggest impressions on me over the years.

Music has a way of not just bringing pictures to the theatre of your mind but bringing you places emotionally. This is the soundtrack to my life so far. Please enjoy it, and let me share the accompanying experiences that make these songs so important to me.

START
Scarborough Fair Parsley, Sage, Rosemary And Thyme 03:14 Simon & Garfunkel
Music Log
Year 1996
Age 1
The mellow vibe and the catchy lyrics make this kinds of folk the best prenatal lullabies for babies. My first time to this world, I listened to a lot of folks and classical music.
Hotel California The Best of Eagles 04:18 Eagles
Music Log
Year 1997
Age 2
This song was a global hit in the 90s, my parents, just like all the people at that time, were zealous about it. Now, I still listen to this song a lot because of the irristable voice and exotic vibe.
La Copa de la Vida Best of 1998 04:29 Ricky Martin
Music Log
Year 1998
Age 3
La Copa la Vida(The Cup of Life) is the official song of the 1998 FIFA World Cup in France. My dad was a super fan of soccer and watched all the matches. I still remember those days I rode on my dad's shoulder, shouting out loudly "Tu y you! Ale ale ale! Go go gol! Ale ale ale!"
Mariage d'amour Lettre à ma Mère 02:41 Richard Clayderman
Music Log
Year 1999
Age 4
Richard Clayderman, Lang lang, Yundi Li and Maksim Mrvica were the "Princes of the Piano". My mom bought me their albums when I was three. I guess my fascination with piano and music can be traced back to that time.
Nightingale Tribute 05:48 Yanni
Music Log
Year 2000
Age 5
After I watched Braveheart, I fell in love with New Age music.I started to listen to lots of new age music made by Yanni and Enya. The perfect combination of electronic music and symphony orchestra created the melodious and exotic vibes.
天空の城ラピュタ~君をのせて 风のとおり道 05:16 高嶋ちさ子
Music Log
Year 2001
Age 6
Though I watched Disney and Pixar's animations way more than other animations, Hayao Miyazaki's animations were my favorite Japanese animations back then. My neighbor Totoro and Castle in the sky were my favorite ones. I also drawed several illustrations of Totoro while listening to the theme songs.
Radetzky March Classic Marches 02:26 Johann Strauss Sr
Music Log
Year 2002
Age 7
Watching Vienna New Year's Concert every year became a family tradition after I started to learn the piano. Radetzky March is the last piece of music during which the audience claps along under the conductor's direction.
As long as you love me Backstreet Boys 03:32 Backstreet Boys
Music Log
Year 2003
Age 8
Backstreet Boys was the first pop music band I loved. Their album was the very first one I bought with my pocket money. Back then I had the luxury of having more time with my music, I would listen to the albums over and over again, memorizing the lyrics and singing along with them again and again.
You are not alone The Ultimate Collection 06:01 Michael Jackson
Music Log
Year 2004
Age 9
This was the first song I cried while listening to. I had no clue why it reminded me of my passed-away grandma, but since then I crazily listened to all of MJ's songs and became a huge fan of him.
Je m'appelle Hélène Je m'appelle Helene 03:50 Hélène Rolles
Music Log
Year 2005
Age 10
This was the first French song I listened to. It blowed my mind when my friend sang the song to me. I immediately fell in love with French which is as beautiful as this song.
Big Big World Big Big World 03:25 Emilia
Music Log
Year 2006
Age 11
The first performance in front the whole class was an amazing experience for me. The age of 11 was a great shifting point in my life as I transfered to another school and became more outgoing and brave. It's not exaggerated to say that this year changed my whole life because of the significant change of my personality.
Petit Frêre Vox Angeli 03:51 Vox Angeli
Music Log
Year 2007
Age 12
After watching the movie The Chorus, the silvery voice from the junior vocal group topped my music list. The voice from the paradise clear my minds and sooth my nerves, which is a perfect remedy for all the stressful schoolwork.
You and Me(我和你 北京2008年奥运会歌曲音乐选集 04:10 Sarah Brightman, 刘欢
Music Log
Year 2008
Age 13
2008 is a big year for all Chinese. Though I didn't have chance going to Olympics, I was still very hilarious watching the opening ceremony at home with families.
The Yellow River Piano Concerto Mov.1 黄河之子 03:30 LangLang(郎朗)
Music Log
Year 2009
Age 14
If you still remember the prince of the piano I mentioned before, I got a chance performing with him for six millions audiences! And this really meant the world to me! I was sitting within 20 meters from him and playing together, I got this album with his autography after the concert. I really think I was the luckiest child in the world.
Waka Waka(This time for Africa Waka Waka (This Time For Africa) 03:19 Shakira, Freshlyground
Music Log
Year 2010
Age 15
I was super zealous about soccer(mostly about the soccer players)during South Africa FIFA World Cup. I was thinking about playing soccer with my friends after school all the day. Sometimes even the bottles on the road made me want to shoot at the goal.
Dream A Good Day 03:30 Priscilla Ahn
Music Log
Year 2011
Age 16
There is a point where the songs in your playlist are no longer your own, but shared with another. Through this they become more meaningful and expressive. This song was my favorite one in the playlist my first love shared with me. It perfectly fits my range amazingly. I sang this song in the audition of the university women chorale and got the soprano position. In my second year in Berkeley, I found this song in the playlist of the guy I liked. This is probably my lovely little lucky song.
Gotta Have You Say I Am You 03:19 The Weepies
Music Log
Year 2012
Age 17
In my sophomore year in high school, I listened to lots of folks, guitar and singing performances and covers. Gotta Have You was the first song that made me want to learn guitar and sing it to my first love. Shame, I put off learning it for years, but I decided to seriously learn it in my third year in college.
Nirvana In the Lonely Hour 03:22 Sam Smith
Music Log
Year 2013
Age 18
As I was more and more open-minded to all kinds of music and voices, the sexy, husky and smoky voice was a mind blowing attraction. Sam Smith, Maroon 5, Say loulou are just the tip of the iceberg. I embarked on a journey to explore not only the popular musicians, but more about the less famous ones.
逝去的歌 10 DAY’S 03:28 旅行团
Music Log
Year 2014
Age 19
I barely listened to Chinese songs before I came to the States until I met Iris, my roommate and my good friend. She is a pretty nostalgic, unique and kind of maverick girl. She loves the songs my parents' generation would like to listen, which are pretty instresting throwbacks.
You & Me (Flume Remix) 04:42 Disclosure
Music Log
Year 2015
Age 20
I started to try things I've never done in my life before: go to frat parties and dance , go to clubs with my friend from Stanford, go hiking in the Tilden Park at midnight and wait for sunrise, try weed chocolate in my first live concert... and start eating apples(seriously, the only fruit I don't eat is apple). EDM and Remix topped my playlists for quite a while. It's quite insteresting to find that my love about electronic music can be traced back to five years old when I started listening to New Age music.

Fin.

I hope you enjoyed this music autobiography. It was quite revealing to me while putting it together. I didn't realize there are so many connections among all stages of my life. Some recent stages can be traced back to those early ones. Some songs were impactful on the course of my life, while others simply got a lot of playtime.

Whatever their role, these songs shaped and described who I was and how I felt at different times in my life. They are special to me, each in their own way.
