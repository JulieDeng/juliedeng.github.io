What is this?

I'm a bird flying independently and fearlessly, singing an indie free of the constraints and norms.

The little bubbles jumping along the curve which portrays the levels of my happiness are the lyrics of this indie song.

I tried to use the least amount of words to catch the moments in my life each year.

Looking back on my past life and creating this interactive experience was a lot of fun. I'm very excited to share it with you.

Thank you for stopping by.
